FAQ

Common Questions

How fast can you arrive after I call?

+
We guarantee a 60-minute response time across San Diego County, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week — including weekends and holidays. The faster you call, the less damage occurs.

Do you work directly with insurance companies?

+
Yes. We work directly with all major insurance carriers and handle the documentation process on your behalf. Our video documentation and itemized reports are specifically designed to support insurance claims and speed up reimbursement.

What is your video documentation process?

+
Every job is documented on camera from start to finish. Our technicians narrate each step — what they're doing, why they're doing it, what equipment they're using, and what the results show. You receive the complete video record at job completion.

Are your technicians certified?

+
All CENTCOM technicians are IICRC certified — the gold standard in the restoration industry. We maintain current certifications in water damage restoration, fire and smoke restoration, and mold remediation.

What does a free inspection include?

+
Our free inspection includes a full walk-through of the affected area, thermal imaging moisture detection, a detailed assessment of visible and hidden damage, and a clear itemized estimate — all documented on video with no obligation.

Do you offer services for property managers and HOAs?

+
Absolutely. We specialize in commercial property management partnerships. We offer preferred vendor agreements with priority response, dedicated account management, standardized reporting, and preferred pricing across your full portfolio.

How is CENTCOM different from SERVPRO or other franchises?

+
CENTCOM is locally owned and operated — not a franchise. We document every job on video, use thermal imaging as a standard practice, and provide itemized transparent pricing. Franchise operators cannot mandate these standards across their system. We can, and we do.

What areas of San Diego do you serve?

+
We serve all of San Diego County including San Diego, Chula Vista, El Cajon, La Mesa, Santee, Escondido, Oceanside, Carlsbad, Encinitas, Poway, and surrounding areas. If you're in the county, we can reach you within 60 minutes.
